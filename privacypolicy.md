# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 17, 2026

### 1. Information We Collect
* **Public Wallet Address:** We collect your public Solana address via the Seeker Seed Vault to identify your account and process airdrop rewards.
* **On-Chain Data:** We view publicly available blockchain data related to your address to verify quest completion.
* **Device Information:** Basic technical data (OS version, device model) to ensure app compatibility.

### 2. What We DO NOT Collect
* **Private Keys/Seed Phrases:** Our app integrates with the Solana Mobile Stack (SMS). We never have access to your private keys or recovery phrases.
* **Personal Identity:** We do not require your name, email, or physical address to use the app.

### 3. Third-Party Advertising & Airdrops
Our Quest platform connects you with third-party advertisers. 
* **Airdrops:** Revenue from advertisers is distributed directly to your public wallet address.
* **External Links:** Quests may lead to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

### 4. Data Security
Your security is handled by the Solana Seeker's hardware-backed Seed Vault. We use industry-standard encryption for any off-chain quest data stored on our servers.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
